Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our drone delivery services

Our autonomous drones use advanced GPS navigation, computer vision, and obstacle avoidance technology to safely deliver packages. Here's the process:

  1. You place an order through our app or a partner retailer
  2. The nearest drone is automatically dispatched from our network
  3. The drone picks up your package from the fulfillment center
  4. It flies directly to your designated landing zone using the safest route
  5. You receive a notification when your package has arrived

Safety is our top priority. Our drones feature:

  • Redundant flight systems that can handle component failures
  • Advanced obstacle detection using LiDAR and computer vision
  • Automatic return-to-base in case of low battery or severe weather
  • Geofencing to avoid restricted airspace
  • Real-time monitoring by our operations team
  • 99.9% successful delivery rate with zero accidents to date

We currently operate in 15 metropolitan areas across North America, including major cities like Los Angeles, Houston, Miami, Seattle, and Toronto. Our service area is expanding rapidly - enter your zip code in our app to check if we deliver to your location. We're adding new cities every quarter and plan to reach 50+ cities by 2025.

  • Priority (5-10 min): $12.99 for urgent deliveries
  • Subscription: $29.99/month for unlimited standard deliveries
